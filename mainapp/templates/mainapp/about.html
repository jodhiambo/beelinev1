About Beeline Challenges

The idea of providing a platform that enables innovators in Africa and LMICs contribute to solving major societal issues has been inspired by the COVID-19 pandemic as we see organizations and governments urgently hunting for ideas and contributions from minds all across the globe.

We have come up with a way of consolidating this information so that it becomes easy for innovators to connect with the required funding, fellow innovators and sponsors. This is a platform designed for innovators, the industry and academia in Africa and LMICs to participate and contribute in generation of solutions and knowledge that impacts the entire globe. We curate opportunities offered by innovation hubs, government and other entities from across the world and you are now able to receive this information from one source.

From the platform, you can also meet innovators with similar interests away from your organization or even country and you can contact them to partner with you. The platform is provided to you by Research Beeline Ltd, a social enterprise based in Juja, Kenya which also runs a funding platform for researchers in www.fundsbeeline.org and for NGOs and non-profits in Africa www.fundsbeeline.org
